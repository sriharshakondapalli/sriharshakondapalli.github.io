Hi, I'm Sri Harsha Kondapalli

I have over four years of experience in RF engineering, ASIC design, Digital Signal Processing, and Ultrasound engineering. Expertise in prototyping energy harvesting wireless sensors and mixed-signal integrated circuits with applications to infrastructure-to-vehicular communication and implantable bio-medical devices. Currently working on my dissertation titled 'Variance-based-Informatics: Theory and design of variance driven physical systems' under the supervision of Prof. Shantanu Chakrabartty and co-advised by Prof. Xuan 'Silvia' Zhang. This research work enables designing low latency and energy efficient wireless sensors using novel architectures called Variance-based-Logic.
